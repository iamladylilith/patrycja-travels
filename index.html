for friends, family & the chronically nosy  ✈

◆

Welcome. If you found this page, I like you enough to let you see all of it — the stunning views, the overexposed photos, the shaky camera work, and at least one incident per trip I'll probably never fully explain.

This is where I archive my adventures so you can live vicariously while I pretend to be a travel influencer — no ring light, no itinerary guides, just me and a carry-on I somehow always manage to overpack.

No brand deals. No sponsored content. (Yet. 👉🏻👈🏻) Just really good trips and a genuine inability to leave anything behind. Enjoy.

My Adventures

Proof that I actually went to all these places

▶
2023  ·  USA

California Road Trip

Two weeks, gorgeous sunsets, LA nights, and one car tow that absolutely nobody saw coming. 10/10 would road trip again, maybe with a better parking strategy.

▶
2025  ·  Thailand

Thailand with Lolek

Three weeks, one best friend, and approximately one million scooter kilometres. The Full Moon Party in Koh Phangan was everything they said it would be and nothing I was prepared for.

▶
2026  ·  Maldives

Maldives

Two weeks of turquoise water, non-stop activities, and a genuinely alarming amount of tuna. Arrived as a stranger. Left with a WhatsApp group I actually check.

▶
2026  ·  Spain

Mallorca with Kasia

One girls' week. Perfect beaches, perfect company, and one tree that had the audacity to win. I have stitches. The tree does not. 10/10 for everything except the tree.
